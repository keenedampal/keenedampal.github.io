Purple

PURPLE!

I really like the color purple. Purple is that one color I could vibe to anytime of the day. Purple gives me that feeling of happiness that is unique from the kind of joy that I experience with other colors and I don't hate it! Also, did you know that carrots used to be color purple and that there is a whole day dedicated to this color? Can you guess what day it is? (Hint: Purple Day is celebrated on March 26!)

The psychology behind purple.

According to the color physcology, purple represents royalty and is linked to power, nobility, luxury, wisdom, and spirituality. However, this color can become frustrating and irritating when overuse. This is why the color purple is often used as accent colors in logos and other projects.
