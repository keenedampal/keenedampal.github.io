Blue

BLUE!

Blue is a very versatile color which could represent so many emotions, moods, and other things! Although I don't use blue on everything, it's still a pretty good go-to color. The blue color gives me a sense of calmness and serenity along with other cool feelings that I love. :)

The psychology behind blue.

Blue is often associated with men but did you know that these were widely used among women in the old days? That's so cool! Also, according to the color physcology, blue is viewed as a non-threatening color with the ability to give off conservative and traditional auras. It is a very helpful color especially when relaxing since it calls to mind feelings of calmness and serenity. Blue is a very beautiful color and it is described by multiple people as peaceful, tranquil, secure, orderly, and a sign of stability and reliability! That's why a lot of security companies use the color blue in their logos when advertising. However, blue is also known for its negative emotions such as loneliness, sadness, aloofness, and depression.



Fun facts about the color blue!

Did you know? The color blue is one of the least appetizing color. Blue is so rare in nature and food, aside from blueberries and some plums, that we humans tend to avoid blue foods. This is because poisonous foods with a blue color could be a very likely sign of spoilage or poison. Some weight loss plans even recommend eating with a blue plate to lessen your appetite!
